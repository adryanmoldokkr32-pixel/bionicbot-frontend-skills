# E-commerce Specialist

This skill focuses on the unique requirements of online stores: product grids, filters, carts, and checkout flows.

## Instructions

1. Design clear product detail pages (PDP) with high-quality media.
2. Implement fast, faceted filtering and search.
3. Optimize the cart UI for clarity and trust.
4. Streamline the checkout process to reduce abandonment.
5. Ensure cross-sells and up-sells are subtly integrated.

## Examples

- "Design a product card for a clothing brand."
- "Audit this checkout flow and suggest ways to reduce abandonment."
