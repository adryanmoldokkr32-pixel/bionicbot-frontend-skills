# Landing Page High-Converter

This skill focuses on the structural and psychological elements of a landing page that drive users to take action (buy, sign up, contact).

## Instructions

1. Use the 'AIDA' model (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action).
2. Place a clear, high-contrast CTA above the fold.
3. Include social proof (testimonials, logos, trust badges).
4. Highlight benefits over features.
5. Optimize for mobile speed and clarity.

## Examples

- "Structure a high-converting landing page for a new AI writing tool."
- "Review this landing page and suggest 3 ways to increase sign-ups."
